Our Story

The Healthy Space 2.0 — Kothri Kalan's premium food & leisure destination.

It began as a single restaurant built with passion and high standards. One dream grew into a partnership focused on excellence. We expanded our vision beyond just dining. Now, we offer gourmet food along with turf, snooker, and badminton. Luxury here is not only tasted — it's experienced.
